## Salutation ##
[User Name]! : Registered User Name Display;
## Content ##
[Birthday/Anniversary]! : Greeting in Email;
## Complementary Close ##
[Restaurant's Contact Name]! : Contact Name of Restaurant;
[Name of Restaurant]! : Restaurant name;
[Address]!: Restaurant Address;
[Phone]!: Restaurant Phone;
[Email]!: Restaurant Email;
[Menu Url]! : Restaurant QR Menu URL;
